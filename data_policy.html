Data Policy for "Analyze your Chess" and "Analyze your Chess Pro" Apps

Advertising

"Analyze your Chess" uses advertising to provide the content free of charge. When you use "Analyze your Chess" app, your device automatically sends certain information to our advertising partner Google AdMob. This information may include for example the name of the app, the device model, the device type, data from sensors in your device, the IP address and the advertising ID. The information gathered is solely used to provide our advertising partners with the information they need to provide their services, maintain and improve their services, provide their personalized services, including content and ads.

Analytics

Both "Analyze your Chess" App and "Analyze your Chess Pro" App use analytics (usage data) in order to understand how users engage with these apps and how to improve them. When you use either "Analyze your Chess" App or "Analyze your Chess Pro" App your device automatically sends certain information to our analytics partner Google Analytics. The information gathered includes, for example, the device type, the session length, and interactions with various user interface elements (buttons) in the game.

The analytics data collected is anonymous and is not personal data.

Opting out

If you no longer want us to use your IP address, then you need stop using "Analyze your Chess" app.

If you no longer want us to use your advertising ID, then you need to delete your device's advertising ID following the steps from here or you need to stop using "Analyze your Chess" app.

For more choices please follow the “Manage Privacy and Customize Ads” link for the respective service above.
